Buttermilk Pancakes

This is a description of this amazing recipe you should totally cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Optional Spices
    • Cinnamon
    • Nutmeg
    • Salt

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Melt butter in pan over medium heat
  3. Spoon a half cup of batter into pan
  4. Flip when golden
